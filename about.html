Our Mission

LaunchPad is a lightweight hub for makers, engineers, and creators. Our mission is to help teams form faster, validate ideas earlier, and fund projects with a mix of preorders and micro-investments.

Built by a small team who believes great products start with great teams.

Preorders Micro-Investments Community

How it works

  1. Share your idea and required skills on LaunchPad.
  2. Form a team quickly by matching people via Skill Link.
  3. Validate demand early through preorders and micro-investments.
  4. Iterate fast with clear milestones and community feedback.

Principles

  • People-first: teams before features.
  • Bias to shipping: small, frequent, and measurable.
  • Sustainable funding: validate demand early, grow responsibly.
